How CampusMarket Works

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on campus

How to Buy

1

Browse Listings

Explore items by category (Tech, Merch, Food, Tutoring). Use filters to find exactly what you need on your campus.

2

Check Details

Click on any listing to see photos, description, price, condition, and seller's campus location.

3

Contact Seller

Tap "Chat on WhatsApp" to message the seller directly. Ask questions, negotiate price if allowed, and arrange a meetup.

4

Meet & Inspect

Meet at a safe campus location (library, cafeteria). Inspect the item thoroughly before paying. Cash transactions only.

How to Sell

1

Create Listing

Click "Sell" and choose your category. Add clear photos (up to 4), write an honest description, and set your price.

2

Get Verified

After submitting, you'll receive a WhatsApp message within 24 hours asking for your DUT student ID photo for verification.

3

Listing Goes Live

Once verified, your listing appears on the site. Buyers can now see it and contact you via WhatsApp.

4

Complete Sale

Meet buyer at your chosen campus location. After selling, mark the item as "Sold" in your Profile to remove it from active listings.

Safety Guidelines

Safe Meetup Locations

  • Campus libraries (best for tech items)
  • Main cafeterias & food courts
  • Central courtyards during daytime
  • Never meet in parking lots or isolated areas

Inspect Before Paying

  • Test tech items thoroughly (battery, screen, etc.)
  • Check clothing for damage or stains
  • Verify textbook editions and condition
  • Never pay without seeing the item first

Bring a Friend

  • For high-value items (R1000+)
  • If meeting someone for the first time
  • When meeting in the evening
  • Always tell someone where you're going

Red Flags

  • Seller refuses to meet on campus
  • Asks for payment before meetup
  • Price seems too good to be true
  • Won't show DUT student ID when asked

Important Reminder

CampusMarket DUT is a platform that connects students. We do NOT handle payments, verify item conditions, or guarantee transactions. All trades are between students at their own risk. Always use common sense and prioritize your safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CampusMarket free to use?

Yes! CampusMarket is completely free for all DUT students. We don't charge listing fees or take commissions. Our goal is to help students trade safely on campus.

How long does verification take?

Usually within 24 hours. You'll receive a WhatsApp message asking for your DUT student ID photo. Once we verify you're a registered student, your listing goes live immediately.

Can I negotiate prices?

Yes, if the seller marked their listing as "negotiable." Contact them on WhatsApp to discuss. Be respectful and make reasonable offers.

What if I get scammed?

Report it to us immediately and contact DUT campus security. While we verify student status, we cannot guarantee transactions. This is why we strongly recommend meeting on campus, inspecting items, and using cash only.

Can I sell services like tutoring?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated Tutoring category. List your subjects, hourly rate, and available times. Meet students at campus libraries for sessions.

How do I edit or delete my listing?

Go to your Profile page and tap "My Listings." You can mark items as sold or delete them entirely. To edit details, you'll need to delete and create a new listing (editing feature coming soon).

What items are prohibited?

Weapons, drugs, alcohol, tobacco, stolen goods, counterfeit items, and anything that violates DUT policies or South African law. See our Terms of Service for the full list.

Can non-DUT students use this?

No. CampusMarket is exclusively for registered DUT students. We verify every seller with their student ID to maintain a safe, trusted community.
